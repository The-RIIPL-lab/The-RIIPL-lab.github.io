List projects

Example 1 Automated segmentation of Nutcracker Syndrome conditions using ultrasound images

Jeongchul Kim is working with the development team to create an advanced AI model that can recognize and categorize a difficult-to-diagnose condition.

Example 2 Creating a shape analysis pipeline for brain regions of interest.

Kedar Madi is creating an automated shape-analysis pipeline to identify significant brain region differences within groups.

Example 3 Looking for voxelwise differences in cerebral bloodflow

Pranjal Rai (Summer Student, 2023) is continuing her work with the RIIPL lab by studying cerebral bloodflow in High School football players.

Example 4 Reducing the electricity usage by the radiology department.

Richard Barcus is developing a monitoring system to assist the radiology department in tracking and reducing the use of electricity by scanners and workstations.

Example 5 Better Image viewing in Radiology Education using the OHIF viewer.

The RIIPL lab is collaborating with IT Services to utilize the Open Health Imaging Foundation (OHIF) Viewer to modernize medical imaging education.
