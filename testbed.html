List projects

Venal Vein Compression Automated identification of Nutcracker Syndrome conditions using ultrasound images

Jeongchul Kim is working with the development team to create an advanced AI model that can recognize and categorize a difficult-to-diagnose condition.

Shape Analysis Creating a shape analysis pipeline for brain regions of interest.

Kedar Madi is creating an automated shape-analysis pipeline to identify significant brain region differences within groups.

Summer Student Project Looking for voxelwise differences in cerebral bloodflow

Pranjal Rai (Summer Student, 2023) is continuing her work with RIIPL by studying cerebral bloodflow in High School football players.

Green Imaging Initiatives Reducing the electricity usage by the radiology department.

Richard Barcus is developing a monitoring system to assist the radiology department in tracking and reducing the use of electricity by scanners and workstations.

Imaging Education Tool development Better Image viewing in Radiology Education using the OHIF viewer.

RIIPL is collaborating with IT Services to utilize the Open Health Imaging Foundation (OHIF) Viewer to modernize medical imaging education.
